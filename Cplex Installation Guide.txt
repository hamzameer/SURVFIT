Cplex Optimization Studio is free for students and academics.  

1. Visit: https://www.ibm.com/academic/technology/data-science
2. Click on Software tab
3. Look for "ILOG CPLEX Optimization Studio"
4. Click on "Register or login to access this software"
5. Use instutitional email to recieve free academic license. 
6. Follow instructions to install Cplex on your system



You need "Rcplex" package to call Cplex optimizer within R. Installation guide can be found here: https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/Rcplex/index.html.
